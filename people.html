John Linken
  • MD & CEO

John Linken has 20+ years of experience in international trading & distribution business as well as extensive knowledge in leading and intergrating multinational teams. Prior to SBL Overseas, John Linken has worked with COFCO International, ADM and Alfred C. Toepfer International covering a wide spectrum of positions within senior leadership.

He is passionate about different cultures, history and agriculture. He enjoys sports like running, cycling & standup paddling, gardening and cooking.

Nick Jonas
  • CFO

Nick Jonas has 30+ years of experience in multiple areas related to finance, banking and risk management. He joined SBL Overseas in April 1995 where he worked in SBL Overseas Chemicals and joined SBL Overseas in March 2007.

Nick Jonas has expertise in finance, process excellence, risk management, banking and treasury, sourcing, marketing as well as business restructuring.

Jerry Bonds
  • Deputy CEO

Jerry Bonds has 30+ years of experience working in a multinational conglomerate and international trading business. His journey with SBL Overseas began in April 1991 where he worked with Jaya Shree Insulators, and joined SBL Overseas in December 1992.

Jerry Bonds has profound knowledge in the commodity trading business, particularly the Energy & Metals sector as well as Strategy and Business Development.
