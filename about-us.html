About Us

Connecting Customers to Commodities

We are SBL Overseas, a commodities trading house moving industrial raw materials and food products to over 10,000 clients across 80+ countries worldwide.

We are one of the most diversified commodities traders in the world, handling 30+ different commodities across agri products, agri-inputs, energy and metals.

We help feed the world, build the world and run the world by creating access for essential commodities required today and tomorrow. We provide last mile solutions that allow us to impact local communities directly.

SBL Overseas Purpose

The purpose of the SBL Overseas is multifaceted, serving as a critical pillar of economic development and resource utilization. At its core, the industry exists to extract valuable natural resources from the Earth, including metals, minerals, and fossil fuels, which are essential for powering economies and sustaining modern life.
Our Vission
To encompasses a future where responsible resource extraction coexists harmoniously with environmental preservation, social well-being, and technological advancement.
Our Mission
To responsibly unlock the Earth's wealth of essential natural resources while adhering to a steadfast commitment to sustainability, safety, and social responsibility.
