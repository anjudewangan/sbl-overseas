Our Services

SBL Overseas PLAYS A CRUCIAL ROLE IN THE GLOBAL ECONOMY, SUPPLYING ESSENTIAL RAW MATERIALS THAT DRIVE VARIOUS SECTORS, INCLUDING MANUFACTURING, CONSTRUCTION, AND ENERGY PRODUCTION. AS DEMAND FOR MINERALS AND RESOURCES CONTINUES TO GROW, IT IS ESSENTIAL TO STAY INFORMED ABOUT THE LATEST DEVELOPMENTS, TECHNOLOGIES, AND BEST PRACTICES WITHIN THE INDUSTRY.

Fertilizer
Fertilizers are substances or mixtures of substances that are applied to soil or plants to provide essential nutrients that promote plant growth and increase crop yields. These nutrients are vital for plant development because they may be lacking in the natural soil or depleted due to previous agricultural activities. Fertilizers are a cornerstone of modern agriculture and are used extensively worldwide to enhance agricultural productivity.
Fertilizers have revolutionized agriculture by allowing farmers to achieve higher crop yields, thus addressing food security concerns. However, their responsible use and management are essential to mitigate potential environmental risks and ensure the long-term sustainability of agriculture.
Energy
We supply large quantities of Thermal Coal, Coking Coal and Petroleum Coke to over 18 energy deficit countries across Asia, Middle East, North Africa & South America. Our customer base spans across large powerplants and cement players to smaller businesses. We provide value added services to end customers such as end-to-end logistics, warehousing, transaction structuring, advisory and price risk management.
Our sourcing is across all major coal deposits across South Africa, Indonesia, USA, Australia and Russia - enabling us to deliver the exact quantity, quality and delivery period required. This also enables us to provide assured market access to coal mines.
Metals
Metals are crucial building blocks for infrastructure development. We trade in Iron Ore and Steel, along with certain ancillary products such as Chrome ore, Mill Scale and Metal Scrap.
We have experience of handling various forms of Iron ore - Fines, Lumps, Pellets, Concentrates and Mill Scale. Our sourcing is spread across India, Australia, Brazil and South Africa.
Logistics
Logistics enables us to fulfil our commitment of connecting customers with commodities. Our Logistics operations range from international shipping and chartering operations to storage and warehousing, thereby enabling the movement of the commodities across boundaries.
We provide bulk ocean freight services - chartering, shipping and risk management - to miners, refineries, producers, traders and industrial manufacturers around the world, to optimally transport their cargos globally.
Agri
We connect producers and users of food & feed around the world through origination, processing, shipping and distribution. Agri offers a range of offerings - Pulses, Grains, Oilseeds, Rice, Tea and Sugar.
Our team is present in all strategic locations - both source and destination - allowing us to provide consistent and diversified market access to suppliers and quality input ingredients to consumers.
Agri Inputs
We ensure the supply of Fertilizers and Sulphur around the world through strong sourcing relationships, distribution facilities and logistics expertise over ocean, river and road.
We support governments by fulfilling tenders and add value to local farmers and retailers by enabling last mile logistics and cargo availability. Our geographical spread helps us secure supplies from fertilizer producers irrespective of regulatory and geo-political conditions, and enables us to continually access various fertilizer markets.
IT
IT services are a cornerstone of the modern industrial landscape, seamlessly integrating technology into every facet of industry operations. These services encompass a wide array of offerings, from network management and cybersecurity to data analytics and software development.
